# Are there other ways to produce a viral song without being a well-known Artist?
In 2023, a small number of songs dominated the streaming charts, with a highly right-skewed distribution in stream counts indicating that most tracks received relatively modest attention while a select few achieved massive success

To really solidify the track's success, the goal should be to encourage as many people as possible to add to their playlists.

So, while your name might have a large influence on how many streams your song will get, try:
1. Focusing on your brand by staying away from collaboartions with other artists
2. Aim to release your song in January
3. Incorporate feels of dance, energy, and valence into the track
4. Work with streaming platforms to increase on platform marketing, through 'upcoming release' promos or recommendation engines.
