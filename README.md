So, while your name might have a large influence on how many streams your song will get, try:<br>
1. Focusing on your brand by staying away from collaboartions with other artists<br>
2. Aim to release your song in January<br>
3. Incorporate feels of dance, energy, and valence into the track<br>
4. Work with streaming platforms to increase on platform marketing, through 'upcoming release' promos or recommendation engines.
